Malaybalay City of Bukidnon Delicacies

Bukidnon, the highland paradise, is the food basket of Mindanao and the pineapple capital of Asia. No doubt that its emerging tasty delicacies come from pineapple. Pineapple pie, tart, jam, piñasitas, and other baked products are now gaining popularity in Northern Mindanao.

Aside from those pineapple-based products, organic rice-based products such as cookies, barquirice, pop rice, chicharice, etc., are also getting famous in Northern Mindanao.
